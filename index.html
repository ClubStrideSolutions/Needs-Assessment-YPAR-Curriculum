Program Phases & Activities

Click on any phase to see detailed information about sessions and activities

Phase 1: Research Foundations April 9-17

Build foundational knowledge and skills:

  • Introduction to YPAR methodology
  • AI-assisted survey design techniques
  • Research ethics and bias mitigation
  • Data collection planning
+

Phase 1 Sessions

Session 1 (April 9): Orientation and Introduction to Research Frameworks

Session 2 (April 10): Ethics in Research and Survey Design with AI Tools

Session 3 (April 16): AI-Enhanced Survey Design Workshop and Tool Development

Session 4 (April 17): Interview, Focus Group, and Data Collection Planning

Key Concepts You'll Learn

  • Youth Participatory Action Research principles and applications
  • Quantitative vs. qualitative data collection methods
  • AI-assisted question generation and survey design
  • Data ethics, informed consent, and bias mitigation strategies
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for qualitative analysis

Featured Activities

During Phase 1, you'll participate in these hands-on activities:

  • Team-building exercise: "What are the needs in our community?" using digital whiteboarding tools
  • Draft survey questions using AI-assisted question generation tools
  • Develop digital surveys with QR code distribution systems
  • Role-play scenarios exploring ethical dilemmas in community research
  • Create data collection instruments with role assignments

Success Metrics for Phase 1

  • Score 80% or higher on research concepts knowledge check
  • Actively participate in all group exercises
  • Submit three AI-enhanced survey questions approved for inclusion
  • Score 4/5 or higher on the ethics assessment
  • Receive positive peer feedback on mock interview facilitation

Phase 2: Data Collection & Initial Analysis April 23-May 1

Gather and begin processing community data:

  • Survey distribution strategies
  • Community outreach techniques
  • Qualitative data collection
  • Basic data preprocessing methods
+

Phase 2 Sessions

Session 5 (April 23): Community Outreach Strategy and Survey Launch

Session 6 (April 24): Survey Distribution and Data Preprocessing Introduction

Session 7 (April 30): Qualitative Data Collection and Initial NLP Analysis

Session 8 (May 1): Mid-Phase Survey Review and Exploratory Data Analysis

Key Focus Areas

  • Strategic survey distribution and community outreach
  • Tracking methods for outreach efforts and response rates
  • Interview and focus group facilitation techniques
  • Data preprocessing, cleaning, and exploratory analysis
  • Initial application of NLP tools for qualitative data

Field Activities

During Phase 2, you'll engage with the community through:

  • Deploying surveys across multiple community locations
  • Conducting interviews or small focus groups
  • Creating and maintaining digital outreach tracking logs
  • Applying basic data cleaning and organization techniques
  • Beginning to identify patterns and preliminary findings

Success Metrics for Phase 2

  • Collect minimum of 30 survey responses per intern
  • Distribute materials at 3+ distinct community locations
  • Submit weekly detailed outreach tracking logs
  • Conduct at least one qualitative research activity
  • Demonstrate ability to clean and organize a data subset

Phase 3: AI-Enhanced Analysis May 7-15

Apply advanced analysis techniques:

  • AI-powered data analysis
  • Interactive visualization creation
  • Machine learning applications
  • Report writing and planning
+

Phase 3 Sessions

Session 9 (May 7): Advanced Data Analysis and AI-Enhanced Pattern Recognition

Session 10 (May 8): Advanced Visualization and Data Storytelling Workshop

Session 11 (May 14): Machine Learning Applications and Report Planning

Session 12 (May 15): Collaborative Main Event

Advanced Techniques

  • Statistical analysis and AI-enhanced pattern identification
  • NLP for analyzing open-ended survey responses
  • Interactive dashboard creation for data visualization
  • Basic machine learning for community research applications
  • Data-driven storytelling and presentation design

Analysis & Compilation Activities

During Phase 3, you'll work with data to create:

  • AI-enhanced analysis of community needs patterns
  • Interactive data visualizations and dashboard elements
  • Thematic analyses using AI topic classification
  • Report sections with AI-assisted content summarization
  • Presentation materials for the Collaborative Main Event

Success Metrics for Phase 3

  • Identify three major data-driven findings using AI tools
  • Create written summary with AI-enhanced insights
  • Develop at least one interactive visualization element
  • Complete one hands-on ML exploration exercise
  • Draft a section of the final report

Phase 4: Presentation & Sustainability May 21-29

Finalize, present, and plan for the future:

  • Report compilation and finalization
  • Interactive presentation design
  • Sustainability planning
  • Career pathway exploration
+

Phase 4 Sessions

Session 13 (May 21): Final Report Compilation and Interactive Presentation Design

Session 14 (May 22): Presentation Rehearsal and Sustainability Planning Workshop

Session 15 (May 28): Unified Sustainability Planning

Session 16 (May 29): Career Pathways and Program Conclusion

Final Phase Focus

  • Integrating all findings into a comprehensive report
  • Designing effective data-driven presentations
  • Developing strategies for continued research and advocacy
  • Creating micro-projects based on research findings
  • Exploring career applications of your new skills

Culminating Activities

During Phase 4, you'll complete the program through:

  • Finalizing the comprehensive needs assessment report
  • Creating interactive presentation components
  • Rehearsing and refining public speaking skills
  • Developing a sustainability roadmap for continued impact
  • Reflecting on personal growth and future applications

Success Metrics for Phase 4

  • Complete final report with integrated AI insights
  • Create interactive presentation elements that effectively communicate findings
  • Contribute to viable sustainability roadmap with action items
  • Interact effectively with stakeholders during presentations
  • Develop a personal career development plan
